Air Fried Chicken Breast

Description

This subtly sweet, yet savory, chicken thigh recipe is crisped to perfection in an air fryer.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • ½ cup maple syrup
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon granulated garlic
  • 4 skin-on, bone-in chicken thighs
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup tapioca flour
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon sweet paprika
  • 1 teaspoon granulated onion
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • ½ teaspoon granulated garlic
  • ½ teaspoon honey powder (such as Savory Spice®)

Directions

  1. Combine buttermilk, maple syrup, egg, and 1 teaspoon granulated garlic in a resealable bag. Add chicken thighs and marinate for at least 1 hour or up to overnight in the refrigerator.
  2. Combine flour, tapioca flour, salt, sweet paprika, smoked paprika, granulated onion, pepper, cayenne pepper, 1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic, and honey powder in a shallow bowl.
  3. Preheat an air fryer to 380 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  4. Drain and discard marinade from chicken thighs. Dredge chicken through flour mixture and shake off excess. Place chicken skin-side down in the preheated air fryer and cook for 12 minutes. Turn thighs and fry an additional 13 minutes.
